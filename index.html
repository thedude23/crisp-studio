What we do for your business

Configuration, design and development of Shopify stores

As partners and certified Shopify experts, we establish a level of excellence in everything we do. Our strength lies in providing our customers with simple, effective and powerful e-commerce solutions that solve business problems and connect brands and consumers through shared values.

Shopify Plus

As Shopify Plus experts, we build brand loyalty and, through powerful SAAS solutions, offer unique and integrated user experiences by designing elegant online stores. We partner with ambitious brands to achieve immeasurable growth through design and technology.

Application customization and development

We help our customers push the limits of Shopify - we have extensive experience integrating custom solutions with complicated requests. Examples of custom applications include product kit or package creation, product or subscription customization, ERP integrations, and shipping service integrations.

Migration of online stores to the Shopify platform

If you decide to get away from your obsolete Ecommerce platform, we are here to help you. With extensive restructuring experience in both Magento and Prestashop, our team will ensure that your transition is smooth and seamless.

Solutions architecture and consulting

We advise and implement strategies to accelerate the performance of your online store by observing quantitative and qualitative knowledge. To keep your store successful and your customers happy we can help you with the combination of our technical knowledge, trends and analytical data.

Continuous priority support services

Our Shopify support services are designed and focused on all kinds of companies, whatever their size. Whether it's a small startup or established global brand, we can and we want to help you. We offer priority assistance for general consulting, development and design tasks.
